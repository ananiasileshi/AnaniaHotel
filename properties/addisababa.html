Addis Ababa

Ethiopia's vibrant capital where tradition meets modernity

Explore Rooms

Located in the heart of Ethiopia's bustling capital, Anania Hotel Addis Ababa offers a sophisticated urban retreat where contemporary design meets traditional Ethiopian elegance.

Our modern architectural masterpiece blends seamlessly with authentic Ethiopian elements, creating a unique atmosphere that honors the city's rich cultural heritage while providing world-class comforts and amenities.

Each of our 120 spacious rooms and suites features elegant furnishings, locally-sourced materials, and breathtaking views of either the vibrant city skyline or the majestic Entoto Mountains. Experience genuine Ethiopian hospitality through our personalized service and meticulous attention to every detail.
