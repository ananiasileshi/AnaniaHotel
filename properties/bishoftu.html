Bishoftu

Lakeside paradise with stunning crater lakes and natural beauty

Located in the beautiful lakeside town of Bishoftu, Anania Hotel offers breathtaking views of crater lakes and volcanic landscapes. Our property combines modern comfort with natural beauty, creating a peaceful retreat just an hour from Addis Ababa.

Each of our 65 rooms features contemporary design, natural materials, and views of the stunning lakes or lush gardens. Experience authentic Ethiopian hospitality in this serene lakeside setting.
