Bahir Dar

Lakeside luxury on the shores of Lake Tana

Explore Rooms

Set on the beautiful shores of Lake Tana, Anania Hotel Bahir Dar offers a serene lakeside retreat. Our property features stunning water views, lush gardens, and easy access to the Blue Nile Falls and ancient island monasteries.

With 90 rooms and suites, each designed with contemporary furnishings and lake views, we offer a peaceful escape perfect for relaxation and exploration. Enjoy our lakeside pool, spa services, and boat excursions to the monasteries.
