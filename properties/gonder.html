Gonder

Royal city of castles and ancient Ethiopian heritage

Explore Rooms

Located in the historic city of Gonder, Anania Hotel offers a regal retreat near Ethiopia's famous castles and royal compounds. Our property blends traditional Ethiopian architecture with modern luxury, creating an atmosphere that honors the city's imperial heritage.

With 80 rooms and suites featuring views of the castle complex or surrounding mountains, we provide the perfect base for exploring this UNESCO World Heritage site. Experience royal Ethiopian hospitality and world-class service.
